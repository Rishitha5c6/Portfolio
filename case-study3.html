PROJECT 3

Developed a responsive e-commerce platform for Varadhi Farms, showcasing a diverse range of organic products such as wild honey, A2 desi cow ghee, and cold-pressed oils. Integrated dynamic forms and backend systems to streamline customer inquiries and enhance user engagement.

Project Overview

The Varadhi Farms Organic Website is a user-friendly e-commerce platform designed to promote and sell chemical-free, sustainably sourced organic products. The site features a wide range of items including wild forest honey, A2 desi cow ghee, cold-pressed oils, and natural spices. With a clean and responsive UI, the platform enables users to browse, search, and purchase products easily. Integrated contact forms allow customers to send inquiries or requests, while backend support ensures smooth data handling and order management. This project aims to support healthy living and rural sustainability by connecting customers with trusted organic products.
